Events Management

We plan, design and orchestrate bespoke weddings, private parties and corporate events for discerning clients who desire the very best for their event in Nigeria, Africe and beyond. Established by leading Event Planner and Strategist Ebenezer Makinde, who with over twelve years experience within the events industry has designed and managed numerous weddings and events around the world. Our highly talented team of party and wedding planners take a passionate approach to each event; we combine our unique designs, flawless organisation, extensive experience and immaculate attention to every single detail resulting in memorable and exceptional events. We ensure the planning process is a smooth one for our Clients, of whom we take careful note to learn their personal taste, personalities, style and inspirations. From the moment you enlist our services to the end of your event we pride ourselves on consistently delivering a high level of service. Driven by our passion for perfection, we strive to exceed our clients’ expectations in all aspects of event planning, design, production and logistical management and have an impressive track record; proven by the testimonials of our clients. Whether you are getting married, planning a special occasion or a corporate event, The event company Lagos are on hand to make it happen.
